The idea

One check-in. A day that fits.

Most planners assume you're a machine with nine identical hours. Akeso plans around the day you actually have — not the perfect day you imagined.

  1. 01

    A 20-second check-in turns into your daily energy score

    Sleep, mood, stress and a few quick signals — no typing, no friction, updatable as the day changes.

  2. 02

    Your hardest work lands in your best hours

    The planner matches task demand to energy supply: deep work in the peak, admin in the dip, recovery on the schedule.

  3. 03

    Meals matched to what your body needs — from your own fridge

    Quick recipes built from ingredients you already have, timed around today's energy curve.

≈20s one daily check-in
6 quick signals, zero typing
0 black boxes — the score is deterministic & explainable
