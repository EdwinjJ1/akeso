# Pitch Team Slide Design

## Goal

Add the supplied team-introduction artwork to the revised Akeso pitch without changing or cropping the artwork.

## Placement

Insert the team slide after **Technical Execution** and before **What’s Next**. This creates a short transition from technical credibility, to the people who built the product, to the closing vision.

## Visual treatment

- Use the supplied 16:9 image as a full-slide visual.
- Preserve its original aspect ratio.
- Do not crop it or overlay additional text.
- Preserve the existing source deck and export a new revision.
- Renumber the final closing slide after insertion.

## Talk track

Spend approximately 15–20 seconds on the team slide:

> Akeso was built by a four-person team covering full-stack development, UI, algorithms, backend architecture, and marketing. That combination allowed us to build both the user experience and the explainable system behind it within the hackathon.

## Sources

The team artwork is supplied directly by the user. All names and roles are treated as team-provided information.

## Verification

- Confirm the image fills the slide without distortion or clipping.
- Render and inspect every final slide.
- Confirm the revised deck contains the speaker note and source block.
- Run overflow and template-fidelity checks.
